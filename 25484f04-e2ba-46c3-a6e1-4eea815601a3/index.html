I regularly

email

Often

‘naysayers’…
These are the
people

stifle
innovation,
are
afraid
of
change, and
are
set on
cruise
control

their
careers. I like
to call these
people
the
C.Y.A.’ers
and I
have
worked with a
lot
of
them- I,
however,
am
not one of
them.
First off

not
condoning
going
off and
being a
‘cowboy’ and I

that rules in

But often

‘rules’ for

marketer, we

always

next
vision,
the
next
way
to
innovate our
marketing to
drive
business
impact. If
you
are
not
doing
this,
you will
quickly
become
an
obsolete
marketer
who
is
back
in the
stone age of
marketing,
strategy,
and
tools
[Like the
companies
who are
just
now doing
‘flash
mobs’].
I
encourage
each of
you to
always
be
working on a
vision
of
what
COULD BE at
your

and

and then
work
to
make that a
reality.Here is
my

creating
marketing
impact &

the

necessary
to
drive
innovation:Develop
a
vision
of
what
could
be
at your

marketing,
internal

We all

‘inner’ circle

check.
If it passes

to a

discussion

your
manager if

to
get
their
buy-in &
support
(never

the value of
air
cover).Drive
that
vision
until
you
reach
resistance,
when
that

have a
conversation

‘nay’
sayer and
ask
why your
vision
isn’t
possibleIF

hit
resistance, try

At the

Stay

Ultimately, if
